The Principles Behind Every Bat

🌲

Sustainably Sourced Willow

All English willow is sourced from farmers enrolled in the UK Willow Conservation programme, replanting 3 trees for every one harvested.

🤝

Master-Crafted, Not Machine-Made

Our 8 master craftsmen carry decades of muscle memory. Premium bats are hand-pressed, hand-profiled, and hand-signed.

🔬

Quality Tested

Every bat is weighed, grain-counted, and balance-measured before leaving our workshop. Bats that don't meet our standard are repurposed, never sold.

📦

2-Year Workshop Warranty

We stand behind our craft. Every bat is covered for manufacturing defects for 2 years — send it back and we'll repair or replace it, no questions asked.
