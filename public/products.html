Grade Guide

Choosing the Right Willow Grade

Not all willow is the same. Here's what the grades mean and who they're for.

Grade Grain Count Appearance Best For Break-in
Grade 1+ 7+ grains Straight, consistent, few blemishes Professional / First-class 6+ hrs knocking-in
Grade 1 5–7 grains Mostly straight, minor blemishes County / Serious club 4–6 hrs knocking-in
Grade 2 4–6 grains Some irregular grains, minor stains Club / League cricket 3–4 hrs knocking-in
Grade 3 3–5 grains Visual blemishes acceptable Club / Junior / Beginners 2–3 hrs knocking-in
Kashmir N/A Harder, denser wood Practice / Training Minimal
